Question generales
Questions générales:
Tu veux sortir ce soir? Do you want to go out tonight?
Tu es libre ce week-end? Are you free this weekend?
Question plus vague:
Tu aimerais faire quelque chose ce week-end? Would you like to do something this weekend?
Invitations spécifiques:
Vous aimez Arcade Fire? On peut aller voir leur concert samedi soir tous les trois. Do you like Arcade Fire? The three of us can go see their concert Saturday night.
Tu connais Daft Punk? J'ai des billets pour le concert. Tu aimerais y aller? Do you know Daft Punk? I have tickets for the concert. Would you like to go?Ça te dit d'aller prendre un verre ou un café ce week-end? How about we go have a drink or a coffee this weekend?
